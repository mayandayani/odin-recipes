German Mulled Wine

Description

A traditional christmas market drink in Germany. A delicious drink to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Wine
  • Citrus juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Any spices

Steps

  1. Mix all of the ingreidents into a pot and heat it
  2. Simmer for 1 hour
  3. Remove pot from heat and let the pot steep
  4. Enjoy the drink! Adjust wine and spices to your taste
